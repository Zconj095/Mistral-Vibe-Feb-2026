Emotional Essence = "The core of who we are emotionally, composed of every positive and negative feeling that shapes our uniqueness and enables deep connection."
Emotional Presence = "The ability to stay fully in the moment and remain aware of your own emotions as well as those around you, which promotes stronger relationships and positive environments."
Emotional Turbulence = "A state of emotional instability that can also fuel motivation when passion pushes us toward action."
Metabolic Stasis = "A balanced metabolic state where energy is used efficiently and the body remains stable despite external changes."
Metabolic Ratio = "The rate at which the body converts food into energy, influenced by emotional releases like stress or happiness."
Emotional Connectivity = "Heightened sensitivity toward others that allows an empath to feel what others are experiencing while also giving compassion and support."
Inner Emotions = "The subjective feelings experienced internally that are influenced by thoughts, memories, and personal history."
Outer Expressions = "The visible manifestations of inner emotions through facial expressions, body language, voice, and words."
Emotional Sensation = "The felt experience that unites emotions, moods, and feelings into a single, sensory-rich impression."
Empath = "Someone who is highly attuned to the emotions of others, often feeling unexpressed emotions and drawn to helping those in need."
